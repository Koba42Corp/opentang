What is OpenTang?

Think Homebrew meets Docker Desktop meets an AI-native App Store.

OpenTang is a desktop GUI app that guides you through bootstrapping a complete self-hosted developer environment — from system checks and Docker setup all the way to deploying production-grade services on your own hardware.

No SSH tunnels. No YAML nightmares. No cloud lock-in. Just click, configure, and run. Your data stays yours.

How It Works

From zero to running in three steps.

No terminal required. No Docker Compose files to write. Just point and click.

1
⬇️

Download OpenTang

Grab the installer for your platform — Linux, macOS, or Windows. No prerequisites needed.

2
🎛️

Choose your stack

Browse the App Store, pick the tools you need, and configure domains and settings.

3
🚀

Click Install

OpenTang handles Docker setup, pulls images, and launches everything. Watch it go.
